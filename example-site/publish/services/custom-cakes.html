Custom Cakes

We build cakes the same way we bake bread — slowly, and by hand.

  1. Tell us the occasion. Birthday, wedding, or "just because."
  2. Pick a flavor. Our favorites: brown butter vanilla, dark chocolate stout, and lemon-poppy.
  3. Give us a week. Custom cakes are made to order, so we ask for at least seven days' notice.
