## We're at the Farmers Market This Weekend

Look for the Hearth & Wheat tent at the Rivermill Farmers Market Saturday
morning, 8am until we sell out (usually by 10). Ida's running the table with
a smaller lineup than the shop: sourdough boules, the cinnamon-walnut
morning buns, and a tray of day-old pastries at half price for anyone up
early enough to beat the rush.

Cash or card both work this year — we finally caved and got a reader that
doesn't die every time it's cloudy.

If it rains, we're still there. Bread doesn't care about weather.
