## Maren Takes Home the Rivermill Blue Ribbon — Again

Two years running now: Maren entered her country sourdough in the Rivermill
Harvest Fair baking competition and walked away with the blue ribbon,
beating out eleven other bakeries from three counties.

Her secret, if you can call it that, is no secret at all — she's been feeding
the same starter since she took over the bakery in 2011, and she still shapes
every competition loaf by hand instead of the mixer. "Judges can taste when
you rush a dough," she says. "So don't."

The winning loaf is back in the case this week in limited numbers, labeled
"Fair Winner" so you know which one to grab.
