# Services

## Daily Bread & Pastry
Fresh sourdough, rye, and seasonal loaves baked every morning. Pastries and viennoiserie available until we sell out (usually by 11am).

## Custom Cakes
Birthdays, weddings, and everything in between. [See our custom cake process →](#/services/custom-cakes)

## Catering
Trays of pastries, sandwich loaves, and coffee service for offices and events. Order 48 hours ahead.

## Wholesale
We supply bread to a handful of local restaurants and cafés. Get in touch if you'd like to carry our loaves.
