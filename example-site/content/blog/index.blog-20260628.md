## Rye Season Returns

Every year around this time we bring back our dark rye — the one with
caraway, a spoonful of molasses, and a crumb dense enough to hold up a week
of sandwiches. It disappears fast, so if you see it in the case, it's not
going to be there tomorrow.

We started making this loaf back when Ingrid ran the ovens alone on Elm
Street, adjusting the molasses by feel depending on how the flour was
behaving that week. We still do it that way. No scale for that part —
just Theo, a spoon, and a lot of muscle memory.

Ask at the counter if you want a heel end for tasting before you commit to
a whole loaf.
